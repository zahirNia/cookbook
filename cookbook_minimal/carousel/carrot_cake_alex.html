Lazy carrot cake

by Alex

Here is carrot cake that tastes very fresh and is super easy to make. Enjoy!!

Facts & Figures

Quantity Difficulty Prep. time Total time
4 portions Easy 40 min 100 min

Ingredients

  With sugar Without sugar
Carrots 200 Grams 200 Grams
Egg 4 Shells 4 Shells
Grinded almonds 100 Grams 100 Grams
Flour 75 Grams 75 Grams
Baking powder 1 Teaspoon 1 Teaspoon
Orange 1 Fruit 1 Fruit
Sugar 200 Grams Use equivalent amount of Agave, Maple syrup, or even Cranberries instead!

Instructions in 4 steps

Step 1

Rasp/grate the carrots so that you have fine little pieces. Add the almonds (grinded like flour). Use two other pots for the eggs. Separate the eggs - white and yellow.

Step 2

Stir the egg white at high speed stiff and fluffy in one pot. Use another pot for the yellow parts of the egg and add the sugar here. Stir it with high speed until it is very creamy. Rasp the skin of the orange.
Add together: The creamy yellow parts of the eggs, the carrots and almonds, the baking powder and the rasped skin of the orange. Stir well. Afterwards you put the stiff egg white under the dough, but don't stir this hard, just use a spoon and stir a little bit until it is all one dough.

Step 3

Put it into a buttered cake form and bake it for 50 - 60 min at 200 degrees celsius.

Step 4

After baking you can cover the cake with chocolate or anything else you like.
