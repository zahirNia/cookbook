Mirza Ghassemi

by Mahin Hekmatian

Here is a recipe of a tasty and simple Iranian dish. It's a sort of Omelette with eggplants. Generally the dish is somewhat flexible-- feel free to adjust the quantities to your taste. I personally prefer the Garlicky version (check out the ingredients section). Enjoy!!

Facts & Figures

Quantity Difficulty Prep. time Total time
4 portions Easy 25 min 90 min

Ingredients

  Subtle Garlicky
Onion 1 Bulb 1 Bulb
Egg 4 Shells 4 Shells
Eggplant 4 Fruits 4 Fruits
Garlic 3 Cloves 6 Cloves
Tomato 6 Fruits 6 Fruits

Instructions in 6 steps

Step 1

Originally the eggplants are supposed to be grilled on fire. This gives them a unique taste. I used my oven instead like below. Let them inside for 45 min at 170-180°C.

Step 2

After 45 minutes, let them cool down. In the meantime, cut your onions and garlic and chop the tomatoes (maybe use a mixer but don't over do it!). Your result should look something like this.

Step 3

Its should be now fairly easy to peel the eggplants off which we do in this step and then using a mixer or a simple knife to chop the already peeled eggplants.

Step 4

Now we start frying everything! Start with onions and garlic, after 5 minutes, add the eggplants. When the mixture got a bit golden, add the tomatoes, bring the stove lower and put a lid on top of the pan. Let it cook for another 10 minutes. You’ll have something like this at the end of this step.

Step 5

Break and add the eggs! Before adding the eggs make some placeholders so they don't spread all over as shown in the picture below. Afterwards put the lid on top of the pan again. Let it cook for another 5-10 minutes.

Step 6

And finally Mirza Ghassemi is ready to serve! Maybe add some parsley on the side to decorate it like I did. Bon Appetit!
