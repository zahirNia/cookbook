Mirza Gasemie

Recipe from: Mahin Hekmatian
Quantity: 4 portions
Preparation time: 25 min
Level: Easy
Total time (preparation and cooking): 90 min

Here is a recipe of a tasty and simple Iranian dish. It's a bit like Omelette but with love, I mean eggplants of course. Generally the dish is somewhat flexible-- please feel free to adjust the quantities to your taste. I personally like the dish with more onions and garlic as you can see in the pictures. I hope you enjoy it!!

Ingredients

Ingredients
What? How many/much?
Bulb Onion 1
Egg 4
Eggplant 4
Garlic Half a bulb
Tomato 6

Instructions in 6 steps

Step 1

Originally the eggplants are supposed to be grilled on real fire. This gives them a unique taste. I used my oven instead like below. Let them inside for 45 min at 170-180°C.

Step 2

After 45 minutes, let them cool. In the meantime, cut your onions and garlic and grate the tomatoes (or use a mixer). Your result should look something like this.

Step 3

Its should be now fairly easy to peel the eggplants off which we do in this step and then using a mixer or a simple knife bring them into real tiny pieces.

Step 4

Its should be now fairly easy to peel the eggplants off which we do in this step and then using a mixer or a simple knife bring them into real tiny pieces.

Step 5

Now we start frying everything! Start with the eggplants, after 5 minutes, add the onions and garlic, after they all got a bit golden, add the tomatoes, bring the oven lower, put a lid on top of the pan and let it cook for another 10 minutes. You’ll have something like this at the end of this step.

Step 6

And finally Mirza-Ghassemie is ready to serve! :-)
